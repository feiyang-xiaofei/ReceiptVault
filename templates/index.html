ReceiptVault Community Edition

Version 1.0.0

A local, offline receipt organizer for freelancers and small businesses. All data stays on your computer — nothing is uploaded to any server.

Features

  • OCR-powered data extraction from receipt photos and PDFs
  • Automatic vendor detection (60+ known stores)
  • Smart categorization with learning from your corrections
  • Multi-currency support (USD, EUR, GBP, CHF, CAD, and more)
  • Duplicate detection via file hashing
  • Dashboard with spending summary stats
  • 100% offline — no internet connection required
